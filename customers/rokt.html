Executive summary

Global ecommerce leader Rokt empowered all employees, technical and non-technical, to rapidly create sophisticated internal tools using Replit. In a single day, 700+ employees globally built 135 fully functional applications—transforming internal operations at unprecedented speed.

The challenge: scaling operations 5X

“We set a goal to remove all friction that prevented us from operating 5x faster.”
— Jon Humphrey, SVP of Solutions and Operations, Rokt

With AI becoming ubiquitous, Rokt knew they would need to operate 5X faster to continue to accelerate its business. To do this, they identified key areas of friction in the business, such as hiring, democratizing analytics and knowledge, friction between their core systems of record and more. Tools like Replit presented them the opportunity to tackle these challenges rapidly.

"Since then, we started to see a lot of our people operations get managed via Replit dashboards. Our financial monthly close is now supported by having Replit. Our legal task trackers get managed in Replit dashboards and applications. And now we're even venturing into the world of actually managing and prioritizing over 30,000 tasks that come through our operations team each year via custom built Replit engines."


Jon Humphrey

SVP of Solutions and Operations, Rokt

Discovering Replit: from notepad to game-changing apps

Jon and his colleague initially discovered Replit on X:

“We stumbled upon Replit and started to build a notepad — that was done in a few seconds. Realizing the potential, we challenged it further, and within a day, built a complex compliance-checking app.”

Why Replit?

  • Fast and intuitive platform suitable for non-technical users
  • Ability to build sophisticated, autonomous applications
  • Production-ready end-to-end software for enterprises

What he discovered changed everything:

"There are only a handful of tools out there, Replit being one of them,

that can truly unlock a team's ability to ship not just internal prototypes,

but actually full end-to-end solutions that can operate autonomously as though they were built by a team of engineers."


Empowering every department

What began as one app quickly became an organization-wide transformation:

  • Operations & Finance: Dashboards for operations and financial reporting; task engines managing and prioritizing 30,000+ tasks annually.
  • Legal: Automated workflow systems, document management, and redlining tools.
  • Engineering: Integrated dashboards combining Slack, internal data, and AWS alerts for anomaly detection.
  • Go-to-Market: Custom CRM tools, LinkedIn integrations, and communication analyzers.

The tipping point: Rokt's global hackathon (“Rokt’athon”), where 700+ employees worldwide demonstrated 135 Replit-built applications—built in just 24 hours.

"We're empowering people who couldn't code with the ability to build software. And it's exciting, having lawyers come up to me and say, 'Jon, I've been building in Replit.'"

Jon Humphrey

SVP of Solutions and Operations, Rokt

Results: a transformational shift

Replit rapidly became one of Rokt’s most coveted tools:

  • Enabled rapid innovation without dependency on engineers.
  • Teams empowered to autonomously develop sophisticated apps.
  • Boosted organizational efficiency significantly
