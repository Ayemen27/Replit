Who we are

We believe software creation should be accessible to everyone,
not just programmers.

Our mission is to empower anyone to bring their digital ideas to life, regardless of their technical background.

We envision a world where turning an idea into working software is as intuitive as drawing a picture or writing a story.

What we're building

We're creating a platform that eliminates barriers between imagination and implementation by creating:

  • Intuitive tools that make software creation feel more natural
  • An Agent that empowers human creativity by handling technical complexities

What inspires us

We focus on inspiring creativity, generating value for creators, and fostering a community where:

  • Software creation is as accessible as using a smartphone
  • Ideas become reality through conversation rather than code


What drives us

Our success is measured by:

  • Empowering more people to create software
  • Democratizing software creation
