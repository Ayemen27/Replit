Your Watchlists

Movie ranking platform combining top movie lists

View App

Views2,384

Description

Your Watchlists is centered around a methodical ranking of the greatest films ever made, carefully calculated by combining 7 of the most respected "Top Movies" lists (IMDB, Metacritic, Letterboxd, and others). The result is our own "Movies To See Before You Die" list that you're free to browse, however as a logged-in user you can mark movies as "Seen" or "Want to See" and thus create your own Watchlist of movies you need to check out. Share that list with others and compare how many legendary movies you still have yet to watch!
